Smart Worker Assistance System

Task Auto-Scheduling

Assigns tasks automatically based on availability, zone, and skill set. Prioritizes urgent tasks to reduce overlap.

Attendance & Check-In

GPS-based automatic check-in/out synced with manager dashboard in real-time. Sends reminders if missed.

Instant Task Notifications

Real-time updates for new or changed assignments, including high-priority tasks and emergencies.

Personal Work Analytics

Displays tasks completed, hours worked, and impact metrics with visual trends over time.

Leave Request Simplification

One-click leave application with reason selection. Auto-suggests alternatives to minimize disruption.

Evidence & Proof Upload

Supports photo, video, or document upload for task completion with location tagging.

Zone & Route Assistance

Highlights assigned zones and optimized routes to reduce travel time and prevent delays.

Peer Collaboration

Worker-to-worker messaging to coordinate tasks, share updates, and resolve issues faster.

Emergency Alerts & Safety

Panic button alerts supervisor and team with real-time location for rapid response.

Rewards & Recognition

Tracks milestones, gamifies achievements, and motivates workers with badges or incentives.

Offline Support

Works in low-network zones and auto-syncs data once network is available.

Training & Knowledge Hub

Quick access to SOPs, safety guides, and tutorial videos for efficient task completion.
